What I do

CAIO leadership sets the AI direction. CISO leadership makes it safe enough to scale.

Chief AI Officer

Turn AI into a managed business capability.

A CAIO role is about more than experimenting with tools. It connects AI goals to business outcomes, sets the operating model, coordinates cross-functional ownership, and creates governance for adoption, training, and risk.

  • AI roadmap and use-case prioritization
  • Governance for approved use, review, and escalation
  • Vendor and model selection criteria
  • Executive alignment on outcomes and resourcing
Chief Information Security Officer

Protect the environment where AI runs.

A CISO function focuses on information security risk, controls, incident readiness, policy, and the liaison work that keeps security aligned with leadership and system owners.

  • Security posture review and risk register updates
  • AI usage policy and acceptable-use guardrails
  • Incident response planning for AI-enabled workflows
  • Control mapping for data, access, and vendor exposure
