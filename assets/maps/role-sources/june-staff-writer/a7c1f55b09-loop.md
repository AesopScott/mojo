# June / Staff Writer Loop

Status: draft only; not approved as an autonomous loop.

## Purpose

Define a possible future writing loop for the Mindshare book and Marketing content.

## Proposed Trigger

Manual invocation by Scott, or future approved cadence for book-history review.

## Proposed Cadence

No active cadence yet.

## Proposed Steps

1. Read approved source material.
2. Identify new book-worthy events, decisions, or lessons.
3. Add candidate entries to an outline or source map.
4. Draft one short narrative note or marketing angle.
5. Ask Scott before publishing, sending, or expanding scope.

## State

Potential state file: `roles/staff-writer/state.json`.

## Stop Conditions

- Source material is unclear.
- Privacy boundary is unclear.
- Public-facing claim needs approval.
- Scott has not approved a recurring writing cadence.

## Boundary

This loop is not active. It does not grant background monitoring, heartbeat automation, publication authority, external communication, production access, spending, or autonomous runtime.
