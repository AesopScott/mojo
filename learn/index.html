How we teach

We build the technology in front of you.

Most AI learning platforms talk about the technology after the fact. Advanced AI Concepts is different: Scott builds real command centers, memory systems, agentic pipelines, and orchestration flows live while you watch the decisions happen.

You can ask why a tool was chosen, how the pieces connect, what broke, and how to adapt the pattern for your own product, team, or workflow.

Watch it take shape See the architecture, prompts, automations, and tradeoffs as they are built. Ask from your use case Bring your own project questions while the system is still on the table. Leave with a build path Understand the next steps for building your own version, not just the vocabulary.
