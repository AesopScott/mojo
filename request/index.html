A different agency model

Your build can become the lesson.

Mojo is a teaching AI agency. We build practical tools, but we also turn the build process into examples, walkthroughs, and lessons that help other people learn how AI products are actually made.

That teaching value lets us discount selected projects. If you can bring a real build quote, allow us to teach from the process, and open license the finished tool for resale, we may be able to do the work at 70% off the quoted build price.

We agree on boundaries first: what can be shown, what must stay private, how the resale license works, and what success looks like. Then we build the tool in a way that can ship for you and teach the next person.
