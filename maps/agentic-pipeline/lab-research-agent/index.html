Lab 1

Research Agent

Build an agent that turns scattered public sources into a cited, decision-ready competitor brief for a customer-facing team.

Source discovery Evidence extraction Citation discipline Confidence and gaps Brief generation Human review
Start the lab Next lab

Customer story

Northstar Solar sells commercial battery systems to grocery chains. Its sales team loses deals when a competitor announces a rebate partnership, financing offer, or installation guarantee before Northstar can explain the difference to prospects.

User

Maya, a regional sales manager, needs a 2-page competitor brief every Monday before pipeline review.

Job

Find credible public updates, separate facts from interpretation, cite the source of every claim, and call out unknowns that need human follow-up.

Outcome

Maya can coach reps on what changed, how it affects open deals, and what questions to ask customers.

Requirements

R1

Accepted sources

Use company newsrooms, SEC filings, utility commission pages, reputable trade press, and customer-visible product pages.

R2

Rejected sources

Do not use anonymous social posts, unsourced summaries, scraped review fragments, or claims that cannot be traced to a URL.

R3

Evidence table

Capture claim, source URL, source type, publish date, extracted quote or fact, confidence, and business implication.

R4

Brief format

Produce executive summary, top 5 changes, customer risk, recommended sales response, and open questions.

R5

Safety boundary

Do not fabricate citations, do not imply legal or financial advice, and do not claim private competitor knowledge.

R6

Human checkpoint

Ask for review before turning research into outbound talk tracks for active opportunities.

MAPS path

Use the APS phases to build the agent one slice at a time.

A0

Align

Create `northstar-research-agent` and define folders, source policy, and class demo boundaries.

A1

Define

Write the brief for Maya, the sales manager, with source authority and escalation rules.

A2

Design

Design the source search, extraction, evidence table, confidence scoring, and brief workflow.

A3

Build

Implement the first slice: accept seed URLs, extract facts, and generate an evidence table.

A4

Equip

Add web search or browser tools, citation storage, and a local output folder.

A5

Evaluate

Test fabricated citation refusal, stale source handling, and missing date warnings.

A6-A8

Ship and improve

Package the lab demo, observe review notes, and add improvements for source quality and brief clarity.

Developer deliverables

Agent artifacts

`agent-brief.md`, `agent-design.md`, `agent-backlog.md`, and one working research output.

Proof

One passing evaluation for citation integrity and one failing example that the agent handles safely.

Demo

A short walkthrough showing source input, evidence table, final brief, and human review point.
