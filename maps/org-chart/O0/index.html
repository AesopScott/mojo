How this becomes MAPS work

Each role can be turned into a first-person role contract, then classified by engagement, authority, memory, tools, and implementation form.

Role contract

Use /role to define the role voice, responsibility boundary, authority, engagement, and proof expectations.

Engagement

Decide whether the role is passive reference, advisor, review gate, workflow owner, operator, autonomous loop, or escalation authority.

Implementation

Map the role to a prompt, skill, hook, script, active process, dashboard, MCP integration, or full agent.
