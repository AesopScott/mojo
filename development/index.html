The process

From brief to deployed in four steps.

We scope, build, and hand off custom AI tools — with documentation and support included.

01

Submit your brief

Describe the workflow problem you're solving, your current tools, and what you need the AI to do. Takes about 10 minutes.

02

Scope and estimate

We review your brief and respond with a clear scope, timeline, and fixed-price estimate. No hourly billing or scope creep.

03

Build and iterate

We build your tool with regular check-ins. You review working software, not slide decks. Feedback drives revisions.

04

Deliver and document

You get the deployed tool, full documentation, and a handoff session so your team can maintain and evolve it.
